Help

Markers

Start Marker

End Marker

You can set a marker from your location, or a place you want to go, and set another marker to the destination you want to go to. You only can set two markers at a time.

Transport

In the Transport tab, you can select from two types of transport, walking and biking, to determine time and routing to get to your location.

Risk Tolerance

In the Risk Tolerance tab, you can select the tolerance for roads you want to take, from safe to risky.

Amenities

In the Amenities tab, you can select from several destination, like grocery stores, businesses, pharmacies, restaurants, cafes, bike shops and repair stations, and many more.

Export GPX File

After you select your route to your location, click on Export GPX File to transfer the information to GPS applications. These include Google Maps, Apple Maps, GPS Maps, Navigation & Traffic, and many others.

Clear

If you want to restart from the beginning, press this button to remove all routes, routes, and deselect all amenities to start fresh.

Layer Button

The layer button is located on the top right of the page. You can change the style of the map from basic, to simple, and real life style of mapping, with each click of the button.

About

The Group

The Route Planner website was produced by group students of the Soft System Class from WMU, by the Client of ModeShift Kalamazoo. People from the group are Matthew P, Christian F, Mike H, Bjarne W, and Austin M.

ModeShift Kalamazoo

ModeShift Kalamazoo is a small community collective that exists to make walking and biking in our neighborhoods a safe and easy transportation choice for everyday activities. This effort seeks to dismantle the stigma around active transportation and work toward a broader mobility system that works for all.
